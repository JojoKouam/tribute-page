Tracy Chapman

Quick recap

Tracy Chapman received a standing ovation at the Grammy Awards on Sunday February 4, performing her iconic song Fast Car, in a duet with singer Luke Combs. This discreet star of American music is hailed for her commitment, the purity of her voice and her folk/rock style.
